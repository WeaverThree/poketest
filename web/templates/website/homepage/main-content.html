Pokémorph Below

Pokemorph Below is a post-apocolyptic sci-fi roleplaying game set in a future where the surface of the planet has been scorched, and the only remaining population lives deep underground in massive cave complexes, all evolved from the surviving 'mon on the surface. Starting from Canal City, one of the most water-rich caves, and branching out to the rebel stronghold of Scrap Town, as well as the Den of Sin Basalt City, life in the underground has to deal not only with the limited resources available, but also the very human elements of greed, lust, and revenge in a world without natural sunlight to sustain them.
